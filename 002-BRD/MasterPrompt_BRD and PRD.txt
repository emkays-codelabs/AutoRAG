## KEY CONTENT RULES

### Pipeline Configurator
- Always described as **model-agnostic** — "Built on AutoRAG's own configuration engine — not dependent on any specific external AI provider"
- 5 questions → YAML config + scoring weight profile + system prompt template + plain-English summary
- Included **free across all tiers** including Open Source
- Available in BRD Section 3.2 and PRD Section 12

### Scoring Formula
```
Score = 0.25 × Retrieval + 0.35 × Quality + 0.25 × Faithfulness − 0.10 × Latency − 0.05 × Cost
```
- Thresholds: ≥ 0.85 = Production-ready | 0.70–0.84 = Needs improvement | < 0.70 = Below threshold

### Rollback
- Auto-rollback triggers: score drops > 0.10 | p95 latency > 5s | error rate > 1%
- Version retention: 30 days
- Manual rollback: one-click from dashboard or `POST /deploy/rollback`

### Approval Framework
- Low risk (top_k, temperature, chunk_size) → Auto-Approve
- Medium risk (retrieval strategy) → Human-in-Loop with 30-min canary
- High risk (model swap, prompt, embedding) → Mandatory Gate — always human

### Financial Figures
- ACV: ₹15 Lakhs / year (~$18K USD) — benchmarked vs Weights & Biases, Datadog, LangSmith
- Year 1 ARR target: ₹1.5 Crore (10 customers × ₹15L)
- Break-even: 8 customers
- All figures labelled as internal projections pending pilot validation
- Market source: MarketsandMarkets RAG Infrastructure Forecast 2023–2028 ($1.2B → $4.7B)

### Efficiency Gains
- Label as **"Expected / Projected"** — not validated claims
- Footnote: "To be replaced with pilot customer actuals post-MVP"
